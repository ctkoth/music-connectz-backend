Music ConnectZ - User Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like your Music ConnectZ account data deleted, send a request to ctkoth@gmail.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request.

Please include

  • Your full name
  • The email address used to sign in
  • The sign-in provider used (for example: Facebook, Google, GitHub)

What we delete

  • Profile and account data stored by Music ConnectZ
  • Connected login records linked to your account

Processing time

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
