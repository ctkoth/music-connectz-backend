Yes, wishlists can be either private or public as a post. Here’s how it will work:

- When a user adds an item to their wishlist, they can choose to keep it private (only visible to them) or make it public (visible to others as a post in the feed or on their profile).
- Public wishlists will appear as posts, allowing others to see what users are looking for, potentially leading to offers or connections.
- Private wishlists remain hidden from other users.

This will be implemented for both the marketplace and lessons features. Each wishlist entry will have a visibility setting (private/public), and public wishlists will be posted to the appropriate feed.

This feature will be included in the v10.1 implementation plan.
